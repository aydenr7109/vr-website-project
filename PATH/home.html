What is Virtual Reality?

Virtual Reality is described by many to be a way of entering a simulated yet realistic world that can be interacted with. Throughout the past century, the idea of Virtual Reality has been expanded and experimented upon to the point of where Virtual Reality has become a wonder of its own. With this website, you will be able to see how Virtual Reality has evolved over the past century. Because of this, an interesting idea that certain people wonder is how and what could be the potential future of Virtual Reality.
