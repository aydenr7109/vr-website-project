What is Virtual Reality?

Virtual Reality is described by many to be a way of entering a simulated yet realistic world that can be interacted with. Throughout the past century, the idea of Virtual Reality has been expanded and experimented upon to the point of where Virtual Reality has become a wonder of its own Because of this, an interesting question that makes certain people wonder is what could be the potential of Virtual Reality in the future.
